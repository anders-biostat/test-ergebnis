You are likely contagious! Please go home, do not come to ZMBH, self-isolate, and contact your doctor by phone.

Important: You are now required to inform the Gesundheitsamt (health authority) of the City of Heidelberg. Call them and tell them that you seem to be infected with the coronavirus (from 7:30 am to 7:00 pm: 06221 522-1881). Also, ask them for an appointment at an official testing station to get the test redone with an approved method.

Since there are only limited official tests available, they might hesitate to give you a code for a clinical test. To get one you need to be convincing. Tell them that you were participant in an experimental test that is being developed at the ZMBH, and that it turned out that you are most likely positively tested. If they are not convinced, give them the number of the directorate of the ZMBH (06221-546814) or even Elmar Schiebel's home phone number (06221-6503382).

Of course, we might have made a mistake. After all, this is an experimental test. So don't panic, but get tested as soon as possible.

Please remember that the test is anonymous, and that we don’t know your identity. If you wish to discuss the result with us, feel free to contact us (Simon Anders, 0176-59535777, or Michael Knop, 01525-4756440), or suggest to your doctor to contact us. However, you may also decide to remain anonymous, and then, it is your responsibility to get this result double-checked. We urge you to do so.
